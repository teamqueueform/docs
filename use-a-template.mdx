title: Templates
description: Use pre-made QueueForm templates.
---

QueueForm offers 16 pre-made templates. Follow these steps to proceed:-

1. Click **Use a template**.
2. Select from **16 available templates**.
3. Preview templates using arrows.
4. Click **Use this template**.
5. Templates can be edited exactly like “Start from scratch”.
6. If no edits are needed, share the link or integrate directly.
