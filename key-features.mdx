## Viral Referral Mechanics
Each signup generates a unique referral link that participants can share to grow your campaign organically.

## Rewards & Milestones
QueueForm supports:
- Milestone-based rewards
- Rank-based rewards
- Random winner giveaways

## Rank-Based Waitlists
Participants climb the leaderboard by referring others.

## Drag & Drop Form Builder
Create multi-step forms using customizable slides without code.

## Real-Time Analytics
Track total submissions, referrals, and referred leads.

## Email Verification
Ensures only genuine participants are eligible for rewards.

## White-Label Customization
Style campaigns to match your brand.
