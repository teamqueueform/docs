### Create Your Account

1. Click **Get Started**, which redirects you to

    `https://app.queueform.com/auth`
2. Login via email or Google
3. Enter the 6-digit verification code
4. Access the dashboard

![Login flow](image-placeholder)

### Dashboard Overview

From the dashboard, you can:
- View total submissions
- Track referrals and referred leads
- Create new campaigns
- Access templates
- Manage teams
- Upgrade your plan
