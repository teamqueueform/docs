---
title: Why Build a Waitlist
description: Benefits of using waitlists with QueueForm.
---

Waitlists help you:

- Capture early interest before launch
- Validate demand through real engagement
- Generate organic buzz using referrals
- Prioritize and reward your most engaged users

QueueForm waitlists convert interest into momentum rather than passive signups.
