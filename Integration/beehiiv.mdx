## Overview

QueueForm allows you to seamlessly integrate with **Beehiiv** so that every valid form submission is automatically added as a subscriber to your Beehiiv publication.

This integration is ideal for creators and teams who want to grow their newsletter audience directly from QueueForm campaigns.

## Prerequisites

Before starting, make sure you have:

- An active **Beehiiv account**
- Access to your Beehiiv **Publication Settings**
- A QueueForm campaign ready for integration

---

## Step 1: Enable Beehiiv Integration in QueueForm

1. Open your QueueForm dashboard.
2. Navigate to your campaign.
3. Go to the **Integrations** tab.
4. Find **Beehiiv** and toggle it **ON**.
5. A pop-up will appear asking for:
   - API Key
   - Publication ID

## Step 2: Generate Beehiiv API Key

1. Open your **Beehiiv dashboard** in a new tab.
2. Go to **Settings**.
3. Navigate to **APIs**.
4. Click **Create New API**.
5. Add a label for your API key.
6. Copy the generated **API key**.
7. Paste this API key into QueueForm.

## Step 3: Get Your Publication V2 ID

1. In your Beehiiv dashboard, scroll down in **Settings**.
2. Locate your **Publication V2 ID**.
3. Copy the ID.
4. Paste it into the **Publication ID** field in QueueForm.

## Step 4: Map Email Field & Connect

1. In QueueForm, select the **Email Address** field from the dropdown.
2. Click **Connect Integration**.
3. Once connected, click **Done**.

Your Beehiiv integration is now active for the campaign.

## Step 5: Verify the Integration

1. Open your QueueForm campaign in a new tab.
2. Submit a **test entry** using a valid email address.
3. Go to your **Beehiiv dashboard**.
4. Navigate to:
   - **Audience**
   - **Subscribers**
5. Verify that the new subscriber appears.
6. The source will be shown as **API Direct**.

---

## What Happens After Integration?

- Every new QueueForm submission is added as a Beehiiv subscriber.
- Subscribers are synced in real time.
- Email verification in QueueForm ensures only valid emails are passed to Beehiiv.

## Troubleshooting

If subscribers do not appear in Beehiiv:

- Recheck API key and Publication ID
- Ensure the correct email field is mapped
- Submit a fresh test entry after reconnecting
