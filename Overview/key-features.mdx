### Viral Referral Mechanics

Each signup generates a unique referral link that participants can share to grow your campaign organically.

### Rewards & Milestones

QueueForm supports:

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Milestone Based Rewards" icon="signal-bars-good" color="#f4f87e">
    Set milestones with criteria. Users can share referrals to reach the next level and unlock rewards.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Waitlist - Rank Based Rewards" icon="ranking-star" color="#f4f87e">
    Assign ranks with rewards. Users can move up by sharing referrals to claim the awaiting rewards.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pick a Random Winner Giveaway" icon="gift" color="#f4f87e">
    At the end of the campaign, randomly select winners from the waitlist to give the rewards.
  </Card>
</Columns>

Ensures only genuine participants are eligible for rewards.
